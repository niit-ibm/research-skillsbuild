## Executive Summary

Analyzed 36 occupations from the healthcare and finance sectors to assess alignment with IBM's AI product portfolio and future strategic offerings. Scoring reveals strong IBM presence in analytics, finance, and HR-related occupations, with significant opportunities in healthcare AI automation.

### Key Statistics
- **Total Occupations Analyzed:** 36
- **Score 5 (Current IBM Products):** 12 occupations (33%)
- **Score 4 (High Potential):** 14 occupations (39%)
- **Score 3 (Moderate Potential):** 1 occupation (3%)
- **Score 1 (Limited Alignment):** 9 occupations (25%)

## Scoring Methodology

### Scoring Scale (1-5)

**Score 5 - Current Tool Available & High Strategic Fit**
- Existing IBM product explicitly mentioned in O*NET technical skills
- Strong alignment with IBM's strategic AI direction
- Immediate opportunity for IBM positioning

**Score 4 - Potential Offering with High Likelihood**
- Strong skill alignment with IBM watsonx capabilities
- Multiple IBM AI use cases identified
- High strategic importance for IBM

**Score 3 - Potential Offering with Moderate Likelihood**
- Some alignment with IBM capabilities
- Moderate strategic fit
- May require product adaptation

**Score 2 - Low Potential**
- Minimal alignment with IBM portfolio
- Low strategic priority

**Score 1 - No Current or Potential IBM Tool/Product**
- No IBM products mentioned
- Limited alignment with IBM strategic areas
- No clear AI application path
